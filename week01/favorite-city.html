My Favorite City

Bulawayo is the second largest city in Zimbabwe. It is my favorite city because of its calm and cultured nature. It has clean streets, a rich history, warm people, and relaxed pace which give it a unique charm that always feels like home.

The spirit of Bulawayo is captured in one landmark of the iconic statue of Joshua Mqabuko Nkomo, a revered national hero also known as Father Zimbabwe.
